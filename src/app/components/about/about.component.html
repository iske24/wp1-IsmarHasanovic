About NZXT

Driven by passion. Designed for builders.

Our Mission

At NZXT, we believe building a PC should be a fun, inspiring, and rewarding experience. Since our founding in 2004, we’ve been dedicated to providing gamers, creators, and builders with tools that combine sleek design, smart functionality, and rock-solid performance.

What We Offer

From award-winning PC cases to advanced cooling systems and intuitive software, NZXT offers everything you need to build and personalize your perfect setup. Whether you're a first-time builder or a seasoned modder, we’re here to support your journey every step of the way.

Community First

NZXT is more than just hardware. We’re a community of passionate individuals who love tech, games, and great design. We’re committed to listening to our users, sharing knowledge, and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in PC gaming and customization.
